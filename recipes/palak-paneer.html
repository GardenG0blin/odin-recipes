Palak Paneer

Description

Palak Paneer is a popular Indian dish of Indian cottage cheese cubes in a mild, spiced smooth spinach sauce. This delicious creamy dish is made with fresh spinach leaves, paneer (firm cottage cheese), onions, tomatoes, herbs and spices.

Ingredients

  • 150 grams paneer
  • 4 cups palak (spinach)
  • 2 tablespoons oil (or half oil & half butter)
  • 2 green chilies
  • 3/4 cup onions (fine chopped)
  • 1/2 cup tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon ginger + garlic paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 8 to 10 cashew nuts
  • 3/4 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon kasuri methi
  • 1/4 cup water to blend spinach
  • 3/4 cup water to cook gravy
  • 3 tablespoons cream (optional)

Whole spices

  • 1/3 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 green cardamom pods
  • 1 inch cinnamon stick
  • 2 cloves

Instructions

  1. Blanch spinach and blend with cashews into a smooth paste.
  2. Heat oil, add whole spices, then saut√© onions until golden.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook until fragrant.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Add spinach paste, salt, and water. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  6. Add paneer pieces, garam masala, and kasuri methi. Cook for 3-4 minutes.
  7. Finish with cream if using. Serve hot with roti or rice.
