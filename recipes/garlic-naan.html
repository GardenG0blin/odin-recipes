Garlic Naan

Description

Naan is a type of Indian flatbread that is leavened and typically baked in a tandoor (a traditional clay oven). It is typically made from a dough using white or whole wheat flour, and is kneaded for a few minutes, then set aside to rise for a few hours. Garlic is a common addition, typically brushed on top before baking. It is generally served with curry.

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 2 tablespoons warm water
  • 3 cups all purpose flour (add 1-2 tablespoons more if dough is sticky)
  • 3 cup plain whole milk yoghurt
  • 2/3 cup warm water
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • Vegetable oil or ghee for cooking the naan

For the Garlic Butter

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt, more to taste
  • 1 to 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro

Method

  1. Add yeast, sugar, and 2 tbsp water to a bowl, then whisk.
  2. Combine flour, yogurt, salt, olive oil, 2/3 cup warm water, and activated yeast.
  3. Mix dough till combined.
  4. Let rise until doubled in size.
  5. Make garlic butter by combining all ingredients.
  6. Shape and cook naan, brush with garlic butter.
